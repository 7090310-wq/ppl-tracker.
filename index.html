Weekly Schedule
Ground Rules
🔥
Warm-Up
5–10 min light cardio + dynamic stretches + 1–2 ramp sets before working sets.
📊
Sets / Reps
3–4 sets. Compounds: 6–10 reps (heavier). Isolation: 10–15 reps.
⏱
Rest
Heavy lifts: 2–3 min. Accessories: 60–90 sec. Respect it — it matters.
📈
Progression
Hit the top of your rep range with clean form → add weight or reps next session.
🥩
Nutrition
1.6–2.2g protein / kg bodyweight daily. 7–9 hrs sleep. Non-negotiable.
💡
If Beat Up
Drop to 3 sets, cut weight 10–20%. Form > ego. Always.
📝
Log It
Use the Log tab on each day. You can't progressively overload what you don't track.
🔄
Deload Every 4–8 WeeksCut volume & intensity ~40–50% for one week, then come back heavier.
